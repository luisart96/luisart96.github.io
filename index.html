My name is Arturo Dominguez, I am a 26 years-old mechatronics engineer really interested in software development, specially game developing and virtual and augmented reality, I feel we have a huge future with these technologies because we already are using them but I believe we do not take advantage of all they have to offer to us.

I have always wanted to keep my knowledge up to date and learning new things, specially technology related things and I have been working in my own at game and extended reality development. During covid-19 quarantine was when I learnt the most about it while generating income with a friend. If you are interested in what I have done in my career, here are the projects:

Two bedroom apartment
Unreal Engine

A virtual tour developed for Windows that shows a two bedroom apartment that was still in construction at the time, it helped the seller to show an unexisting home to potential customers. Developed in Unreal Engine 4. 3D models made entirely by me.

Three bedroom apartment
Unity 3D

A virtual tour developed for Windows, showing a three bedroom apartment with the same purpose as the previous one. Here we used Unity, most of the 3D models made by me, a few of them were downloaded from the internet. This project was also developed in virtual reality for Android.

Four bedroom house
Unity 3D

A virtual tour developed for Windows. This is an entire three floor house. Here we used Unity, most of the 3D models made by me, a few of them were downloaded from the internet. This project was also developed in virtual reality for Android.

Residential Development
Unreal Engine

This is the biggest project we made, it is an entire residential development, with three different house types and recreation areas. Made in Unreal Engine 4 and 3D models made by a coworker and by me.

Virtual Local Bazaar
Unreal Engine

This is my favorite of all, it is a mobile app developed in Unreal Engine 4, due covid-19, several local businesses were severely affected because they used to go to local bazaar or small markets to sell their products and when covid arrived, all those bazaars were cancelled, our purpose was help people show their brand and products through our app without having to go out and risk your health.
