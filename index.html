My name is Arturo Dominguez, I am a 26 year-old mechatronics engineer very interested in software development, specially game developing as well as virtual and augmented reality.

I believe there is a huge future with these technologies since we as a society are nowadays using them, however I do believe we are not still taking full advantage of what they have to offer us.

I am always interested in keeping my knowledge up to date and learning new things, specially technology related. Specifically during Covid-19 quarantine, I took the time to learn on my own, work and launch my own startup for game and extended reality development.

If you are interested in what I have done in my career, please feel free to review my projects below.

Two bedroom apartment
Unreal Engine

A virtual tour developed for Windows that shows a two bedroom apartment that was still in construction at the time, it helped the seller to show an unexisting home to potential customers. Developed in Unreal Engine 4. 3D models made entirely by me.

Three bedroom apartment
Unity

A virtual tour developed for Windows, showing a three bedroom apartment with the same purpose as the previous one. This project was developed with Unity, most of the 3D models were made by me, and a few downloaded from the internet. This project was also developed in virtual reality for Android.

Four bedroom residential
Unity 3D

Residential Development
Unreal Engine

This is the biggest project made by our startup, which shows a complete residential development, with three different house types and recreation areas. Designed and developed in Unreal Engine 4 and 3D models made from scratch by us.

Virtual Local Bazaar
Unreal Engine

Favourite project made by our startup, which shows a mobile app developed in Unreal Engine 4. Due to covid-19 limitations, several local businesses were economically affected, as they were denied from selling in physical bazaars or small markets. Our purpose was to help these businesses show their brand and sell their products through our digital market app without the need to go out and risk health.
